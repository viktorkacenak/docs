Fayne prices courses individually. Each course in your academy is either free or has a one-time price. There is no single academy-wide pricing switch: you decide course by course, so you can mix free and paid courses in the same academy. You set a course's price in that course's **Access** settings, and you can change it at any time.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Free">
    A free course has no price and no checkout step. Students enroll and go straight into the course.

    **How it works:**

    Set the course to free in its **Access** settings. No Stripe connection is required for free courses. When a student clicks **Enroll**, they are taken directly into the course.

    **What you need:**

    - No Stripe account required
    - No checkout configuration

    **Good to know:**

    Free courses work well as a lead magnet, a value-add for coaching clients, or an introductory course that leads into your paid ones. Whether your academy is open to anyone or invite only is a separate setting (see below).

    <Tip>
      Use a free introductory course to build trust before pointing students toward a paid flagship course in the same academy.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="One-time price">
    A paid course has a one-time price. Students pay once and get permanent access to that course.

    **How it works:**

    Set a price in the course's **Access** settings. Students who click **Enroll** on a paid course are sent through Stripe Checkout. After payment, their enrollment is activated immediately and does not expire.

    **What you need:**

    - A paid plan (Pro or Scale); free and trial academies cannot connect Stripe
    - A connected Stripe account (**Settings \> Payments**)
    - A price set on the course (prices start at a $1 minimum, stored in cents, e.g. 4900 = $49.00)

    **Good to know:**

    You can mix free and paid courses in the same academy. Fayne takes a platform fee per sale based on your plan (3% on Pro, 0% on Scale), and connecting Stripe to sell requires a paid plan (Pro or Scale); see [Stripe Connect](/payments/stripe-connect).

    <Tip>
      One-time pricing fits courses at different price points for different audiences, for example a free "Getting Started" course leading into a paid flagship course.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Course pricing vs academy access

Two settings work together but are separate:

- **Course pricing** decides what each course costs: free or a one-time price.
- **Academy access** decides who can join your academy at all: **Open** (anyone can sign up) or **Invite only** (students join by invitation). You set this in **Settings**.

Paid courses require an **Open** academy: invite-only academies support free courses only. Aside from that, course pricing and academy access are set separately.

## Comparing course pricing

|  | Free | One-time price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Stripe required | No | Yes |
| Student pays | Never | Once per course |
| Access duration | Permanent | Permanent |
| Best for | Lead magnets, client value-adds | Flagship courses, paid catalog |

## Changing a course's price

You can change a course's price at any time in its **Access** settings. Students who have already paid keep their access; a price change only affects new purchases. Switching a paid course to free lets new students enroll without paying, and does not affect existing purchasers.

<Info>
  After changing pricing, double-check the course's Access settings. A course with no price set is free.
</Info>
