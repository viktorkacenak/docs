Fayne uses Stripe Connect to process payments, which means every payment a student makes goes directly into your Stripe account. Fayne is never in the money flow — there is no middleman holding your revenue or batching payouts. When you connect your Stripe account, you own the full payment relationship with your students.

## Connect your Stripe account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings > Payments">
    In your owner dashboard, open **Settings** and click the **Payments** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Stripe">
    Click the **Connect Stripe** button. You will be redirected to Stripe's secure onboarding flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe onboarding">
    Stripe will ask for your business details, bank account, and identity verification. This is Stripe's standard process and is required to enable payouts. The steps vary depending on your country and business type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to your Fayne dashboard">
    Once you finish onboarding, Stripe redirects you back to your dashboard automatically. Your account appears as connected, and you are ready to accept payments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You only need to connect Stripe if you plan to charge for your academy. If your access model is set to **Free**, you can skip this step entirely.
</Note>

## How Stripe Checkout works

When a student pays for a course or subscribes, they go through Stripe Checkout — a hosted payment page served directly by Stripe. You do not build or maintain a payment form. Stripe handles card validation, 3D Secure authentication, and international card support automatically.

The checkout page displays your academy's primary brand color, so it feels consistent with the rest of your academy even though it is hosted on Stripe's infrastructure.

## Subscription management via Stripe Customer Portal

For students on a full-access subscription, Stripe's Customer Portal handles self-service billing management. Students can access the portal through a link inside your academy. From the portal they can:

- View their billing history and download invoices
- Update their payment method
- Cancel or pause their subscription

You do not need to build any of this yourself. Stripe manages the portal, and any changes students make are reflected in your Stripe dashboard immediately.

## Failed payment alerts

If a student's payment fails — whether for a one-time purchase or a recurring subscription — you will receive a failed payment alert.

<Warning>
  Stripe sends automatic email alerts to both you and the student when a payment fails. For subscriptions, Stripe retries the charge according to your retry settings before marking the subscription as past due. Check your Stripe dashboard's **Radar** and **Billing** settings to configure how many retries occur and how long before access is affected.
</Warning>
