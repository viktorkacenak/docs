Fayne ships with a curated library of professional business courses, organized into thematic Tracks. You do not start from a blank page. Every course in the library is fully written, structured, and ready to use. Your job is to pick what fits your audience, add it to your academy, and customize from there.

## How content is structured

Content in Fayne follows a four-level hierarchy:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lesson" icon="file-text">
    The atomic unit. Each lesson is 10 to 20 minutes of focused content. Lessons are reusable across many courses.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Course" icon="book-open">
    A sequence of 5 to 15 lessons in a logical progression. For example, "Cold Outreach Mastery."
  </Card>

  <Card title="Track" icon="layers">
    A group of 3 to 7 related courses covering a domain, such as the Sales Mastery Track or the Leadership Track.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Academy" icon="graduation-cap">
    Your complete branded product. What students see as one unified destination at your URL.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Browse and add courses from the library

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course library">
    In your owner dashboard, click **Courses** in the left sidebar, then select **Browse library**. Courses are organized by Track. You can filter by Track or search by keyword.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview a course before adding it">
    Click any course to open its detail view. You will see the full lesson outline, estimated duration, and lesson count. Scroll through the outline to confirm the course fits your audience before committing.

    <Tip>
      Preview a few courses in the same Track to compare outlines. Tracks are designed to work together, but you can add individual courses without adding the entire Track.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a course to your academy">
    Click **Add to my academy** on any course detail page. The course appears in your academy immediately. Students can see and enroll in it once you publish it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an entire Track at once">
    To add all courses in a Track in one action, open the Track view and click **Add all courses**. Each course is added individually so you can remove specific ones afterward if needed.

    <Note>
      Bulk-adding a Track adds the courses as they exist today. New courses added to the Track in the future are not added automatically.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The master library and your copy

When you add a course, Fayne copies its content into your academy as your own private version. This is what lets you customize freely.

Here is how it works:

- **Adding a course copies its content into your academy.** From that moment, every lesson is your own copy. You edit it in place, by hand or with AI.
- **Your edits never touch the master library or any other academy.** Each academy has its own independent copies.
- **Master improvements apply to future additions only.** When Fayne improves a course in the master library, those updates flow into academies that add the course afterward. Courses already in your academy are not changed.

<Info>
  Copies are made per lesson when you add a course. Every lesson in an added course is an independent copy that you own and can edit.
</Info>

This gives you professionally authored content as a starting point, with the freedom to make any lesson sound exactly like you.

## Build a custom course from individual lessons

You are not limited to the pre-built courses in the library. You can pick any lessons from anywhere in the library and arrange them into a new course that fits your exact curriculum.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new course">
    In your **Courses** section, click **New course**. Give it a name and an optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add lessons from the library">
    Click **Add lessons**. Browse or search the full lesson library across all Tracks. Select any lessons you want to include. There is no restriction on mixing lessons from different Tracks or courses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange lessons with drag and drop">
    Once lessons are added, drag them into the order that makes sense for your audience. The lesson list supports drag-and-drop reordering.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set pricing and publish">
    Assign a price to the course (or mark it free) and publish it. Students can now enroll.

    <Tip>
      Custom courses are a powerful way to build a signature curriculum. For example, pull the negotiation lessons from Sales, the communication lessons from Leadership, and the decision-making lessons from Operations into one course your competitors cannot replicate.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I write a lesson from scratch?">
    Yes. Inside any course, click **New lesson** to open the block editor with a blank canvas. You can write entirely original lessons, use AI to help draft them, and mix them freely with library lessons in the same course.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my edits if Fayne improves the master?">
    Nothing. The lessons in your academy are your own copies. Improvements Fayne makes to the master library apply only to courses added after the update. Courses already in your academy stay exactly as you left them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove a course from my academy?">
    Yes. Open the course in your dashboard and select **Remove from academy**. Students who are already enrolled retain access to their completed lessons.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
