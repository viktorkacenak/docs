Your branding settings control everything students see: the name and tagline on your landing page, your logo in the header, your color scheme throughout the site, and the sender name on every email your academy sends. Every surface is yours. There is no "Powered by Fayne" label visible to students anywhere in the experience.

## What you can configure

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Academy name and tagline" icon="type">
    The name and tagline appear on your public landing page and in the browser tab. Your tagline is the short line that appears beneath your academy name.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Logo" icon="image">
    Upload a separate logo for light mode and dark mode. Accepted formats are PNG, SVG, and JPG. Your logo appears in the site header and on authentication screens.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Primary and accent colors" icon="palette">
    Your primary color drives buttons, links, and key UI highlights. Your accent color is used for secondary highlights and hover states. Both colors are applied consistently across your academy and Stripe Checkout.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Light and dark mode" icon="sun-moon">
    Both modes are fully branded. Students can switch between them. Your logos for each mode are shown independently, so you can use a dark logo on a white background and a light logo on a dark background.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Set up your branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Branding settings">
    In your owner dashboard, click **Branding** in the left sidebar. You will see a live preview of your academy on the right side of the screen that updates as you make changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your academy name and tagline">
    Enter your academy name and a short tagline. The tagline appears directly beneath your name on the public landing page. Keep it to one sentence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Click the logo upload area and select your image file. If you want a different logo to appear in dark mode (for example, a white version of your logo), upload it in the **Dark mode logo** field.

    <Tip>
      SVG files scale cleanly at any size. If you have an SVG version of your logo, use it here.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your primary and accent colors">
    Click each color swatch to open the color picker. You can enter a hex code directly if you want an exact match to your brand guidelines. The live preview updates immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your headline and description">
    Your headline is the large text displayed on your academy landing page below the logo. Your description appears beneath it. Both support plain text only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your email sender identity">
    Scroll to the email settings section. Enter the sender name your students will see in their inbox (for example, "The Cold Outreach Academy" or "Viktor at Sales Academy"). Transactional emails including signup confirmations, magic links, and course access notifications go out under this identity, not Fayne's.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and preview">
    Click **Save changes**. Use the live preview panel or open your academy URL to see the branding applied in full.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your academy URL

When you set up branding, your academy is accessible at a subdomain in the format:

```
yourname.academylive.co
```

You choose the subdomain during onboarding, and you can change it at any time in **Settings > Domain**. The subdomain powers your student landing page, your course detail pages, and your authentication screens.

<Note>
  If you want to use a fully custom domain (for example, `learn.yourcompany.com`), see the [Custom Domain](/academy/custom-domain) guide.
</Note>

## White-label guarantee

No Fayne branding appears anywhere in the student experience. Your students see your name in the header, your colors throughout the UI, your logo on every screen, and your sender name in their inbox. The platform is invisible.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use different colors for light and dark mode?">
    Your primary and accent colors are applied in both modes. The difference between modes is your logo: you can upload a separate light-mode and dark-mode version so the logo always reads clearly against the background.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What email flows use my sender identity?">
    All transactional emails use your sender identity: signup confirmation, magic link login, password reset, one-time passcode, course assigned, course access revoked, and academy access removed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I update my branding after I launch?">
    Yes. Changes take effect immediately. Students who reload the page will see the updated branding. There is no re-publishing step required for branding changes alone.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
