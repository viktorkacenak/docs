Fayne Academy is built around one idea: you should never start from scratch. Instead of handing you a blank editor and a list of features, Fayne gives you a curated library of professionally written business courses. You pick what fits your audience, make it yours, set a price, and launch — all without writing a single lesson from zero.

## Who Fayne Academy is for

Fayne is designed for three groups of people:

- **Coaches and consultants** who want to package their expertise into a scalable digital product. You bring the audience and the niche; Fayne brings the curriculum. Common examples: a sales coach launching a cold outreach academy, a leadership consultant building a first-time manager program, or a fractional CMO creating a B2B marketing school.
- **Agencies** looking to add a white-labeled academy to every client engagement — either as a retainer value-add or a separate product line. Each client gets their own independently branded academy from one account.
- **SaaS companies** running customer education portals. Mix Fayne's general business courses with your own product tutorials to drive onboarding, adoption, and retention.

## The four content levels

Everything in Fayne Academy is organized in a four-level hierarchy. Understanding it helps you think about how your academy is structured and how much flexibility you have.

| Level | What it is | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Lesson** | The atomic unit. One focused topic, 10-20 minutes. | "How to write a cold email subject line" |
| **Course** | 5-15 lessons in a logical progression. | "Cold Outreach Mastery" |
| **Track** | Related courses covering a full domain. | "Sales Mastery Track" |
| **Academy** | Your complete branded product. What students see. | "The Cold Outreach Academy" |

Lessons are modular and reusable. Hundreds of library lessons can be combined into thousands of unique course configurations, so you are never stuck with a fixed catalog.

## The Explore library and your copy

All pre-made content lives in a central Explore library that Fayne maintains and improves over time. When you add a course to your academy, Fayne copies that content into your academy as your own private version.

Here is what that means:

- **The content is yours from the moment you add it.** Adding a course copies every lesson into your academy. You can edit any lesson freely, by hand or with AI, without affecting the Explore Library or any other academy.
- **Your edits are always safe.** Because each lesson is your own copy, nothing you change is ever overwritten.

This gives you the polish of professionally authored content as a starting point, with the freedom to make every lesson sound like you.
