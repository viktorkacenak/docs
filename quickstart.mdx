The whole onboarding is designed to get you from signup to a live academy in under 15 minutes. Each step is focused on one decision: what to call your academy, which courses to include, how it looks, how you charge for it, and finally, launching it. By the end of step five you have a real URL you can share.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Name your academy">
    Give your academy a name. Fayne generates a subdomain automatically based on what you type — for example, "Cold Outreach Academy" becomes `cold-outreach-academy.academylive.co`. You can edit the subdomain before moving on, or leave the suggestion as-is and change it later.

    <Tip>
      Pick a name that reflects your niche and audience. This is what students see in the browser tab, in emails, and on your landing page. You can set a custom domain after launch.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your courses">
    Browse the course library and add what fits your audience. Courses are organized into Tracks — themed groups like Sales, Marketing, Leadership, and Operations — so you can scan by domain rather than scrolling an endless list.

    Each course shows the lesson count, total estimated duration, and a full outline. Preview any course before adding it. When you're ready, add it to your academy in one click. You can add as many or as few courses as you want at this stage, and adjust the lineup any time after launch.

    <Info>
      You can also add a full Track in one click to populate your academy quickly, then remove individual courses that don't fit.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make it yours">
    Upload your logo, pick a primary color, and choose an accent color. A live preview on the right side of the screen updates in real time so you can see exactly what your academy looks like to students as you make changes.

    This is the moment your academy stops looking like a template and starts looking like your brand. The student experience is fully white-labeled — your subdomain, your logo, your colors, your email sender. Students never see the Fayne name.

    <Note>
      You don't need a finished logo or a final color palette to get through onboarding. You can update branding at any time from your dashboard.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your pricing">
    Choose how you want to charge for access. Fayne supports three models:

    - **Free** — open access, no payment required. Good for lead generation or community building.
    - **Per course** — students pay for individual courses. Good for a la carte offerings or tiered products.
    - **Subscription** — students pay a recurring fee for full access to your academy. Good for ongoing learning programs.

    Payments run through your own Stripe account via Stripe Connect. You connect your Stripe account during this step and money goes directly to you — Fayne never holds your revenue.

    <Tip>
      Not sure which model to start with? Most coaches begin with per-course pricing. It is the lowest-friction option and gives you data on which courses your audience values most.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go live">
    Review a full preview of your academy — the landing page, course catalog, and course detail pages — exactly as your students will see them. When you're happy with it, click **Launch**.

    Fayne gives you a shareable link you can drop in an email, a social post, or a DM. Your academy is live at your subdomain and ready to accept students.

    <Info>
      You start a 7-day free trial when you launch. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel any time before that.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
