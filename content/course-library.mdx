The course library is Fayne's catalog of professionally written business courses. Every course is organized into a Track, a themed collection of related courses covering a domain like Sales, Marketing, Leadership, or Operations. You start here when setting up your academy, and you can return at any time to add more courses as your audience grows. The core promise of the library is that the content is already done: you browse, preview, and add, rather than write from scratch.

## How courses are organized

The library uses a four-level hierarchy:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lesson" icon="file-text">
    The atomic unit. Each lesson covers one focused topic and takes 10 to 20 minutes to complete. Lessons are reusable across many courses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Course" icon="book-open">
    A sequence of 5 to 15 lessons with a clear progression. For example: "Cold Outreach Mastery."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track" icon="layers">
    A collection of 3 to 7 related courses on one domain. For example: "Sales Mastery Track" groups cold outreach, discovery calls, and closing courses together.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Academy" icon="graduation-cap">
    Your complete branded product. It is what students see as one unified destination at your subdomain.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Browsing the library

Navigate to **Library** from your academy dashboard. Courses are grouped by Track. For each course you can see:

- **Lesson count**: how many lessons are in the course
- **Estimated duration**: total time to complete the course
- **Full outline**: all lesson titles in order
- **Preview**: open a read-only view of the lesson content before adding

Use the Track view to see how multiple courses fit together as a curriculum. If a Track aligns with your audience, you can add all its courses at once.

## Adding courses to your academy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    Open the library and browse by Track, or use the search bar to find a specific topic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview before adding">
    Click any course to see the full outline. Click any lesson title to preview its content before committing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a single course">
    Click **Add to my academy** on any course. The course appears in your course list immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a full Track">
    On the Track overview page, click **Add Track** to add all courses in that Track at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Preview a few lessons in a course before adding it. This helps you confirm the tone and depth match your audience's expectations before you customize or publish.
</Tip>

## Building a custom course

You are not limited to the pre-made courses in the library. You can assemble a course from any combination of individual lessons drawn from anywhere in the library.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new course">
    Go to **Courses** and click **New course**. Give it a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick lessons from the library">
    Use the lesson picker to browse the full lesson catalog. Select any lessons you want, regardless of which course or Track they originally belong to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange the order">
    Drag and drop the selected lessons into the order you want. The course structure is yours to define.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set pricing and publish">
    Name the course, set a price (free, one-time, or subscription access), and publish it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  A single lesson can appear in multiple courses. For example, a lesson on writing subject lines might appear in your cold email course and your copywriting course at the same time.
</Info>

## The master library and automatic updates

When you add a course from the library, your academy gets a reference to the master version of each lesson. Master lessons are maintained and improved over time. As long as you have not edited a lesson, it stays in sync with the master and receives those updates automatically.

When you edit a lesson (manually or with AI), Fayne creates a fork: your own private copy of that lesson. Forked lessons:

- Carry a **Customized** badge in the editor sidebar
- No longer receive automatic updates from the master
- Can be reset to the master version at any time from the lesson settings

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Unedited lessons" icon="refresh-cw">
    Stay linked to the master. Updates from the content team flow in automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Forked lessons" icon="git-branch">
    Your own copy, shaped to your voice. Frozen at the version you edited. You control when or whether to reset.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Resetting a forked lesson to the master version overwrites your edits. This cannot be undone. If you want to keep your customizations as a reference, copy the content before resetting.
</Warning>
